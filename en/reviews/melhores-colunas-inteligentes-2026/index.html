Introduction

Choosing the right smart speaker can be tricky. Amazon, Google and Apple compete in this market with different proposals, each with its own strengths. In this review, we compare the main models to help you decide which one to buy in 2026.

Amazon Echo Dot 5th generation

The Amazon Echo Dot 5th generation continues to be the best-selling smart speaker in Portugal, and for good reason.

Pros: - Unbeatable price (€35-50 on sale) - Excellent sound quality for the size - Integrated temperature sensor - Alexa in Portuguese from Portugal - Thousands of Skills available - Compatible with most smart home devices

Cons: - Sound does not fill large rooms - Simple design (fabric only) - Privacy: microphones always listening

Google Nest Audio

The Google Nest Audio is the direct alternative to the Echo Dot, focusing on sound quality.

Pros: - Best sound in the category (50W vs 30W of Echo Dot) - Premium design in recycled fabric - Integration with Google services (Calendar, Maps, Gmail) - Assistant in Portuguese

Cons: - More expensive (70-90€) - Fewer compatible smart home devices - Display only available on Nest Hub

Apple HomePod Mini

For those already invested in the Apple ecosystem, the HomePod Mini is the natural choice.

Pros: - Surprisingly good sound for the size - Seamless integration with iPhone, iPad and Mac - Siri works well with HomeKit - Compact and elegant design

Cons: - High price (100-110€) - Limited to Apple users - Less smart home compatibility

Which one to choose depending on your ecosystem

  • Amazon Prime user — Echo Dot or Echo Studio (better integration with Prime Music, Video and purchases)
  • Google user — Nest Audio (Calendar, Gmail, Maps integrated)
  • Apple User — HomePod Mini (AirPlay, Handoff, HomeKit)
  • You just want good sound — Echo Studio (best overall sound)
  • Limited budget — Echo Dot 5th gen (best value for money)

Final verdict

The Amazon Echo Dot 5th generation is our choice for most people. It offers the best balance between price, features and sound quality.

If sound is your top priority, the Google Nest Audio or Echo Studio are better choices. For Apple users, the HomePod Mini is worth the extra investment.

Recommendation: Start with an Echo Dot and expand as needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best smart speaker for beginners?
The Amazon Echo Dot 5th generation is the best option for value for money, with good sound and access to thousands of skills.
Google Nest or Amazon Echo: which one to choose?
Choose Google Nest if you use Google services, Amazon Echo if you use Prime and Alexa. Both are excellent.
Is it worth buying a speaker with a screen?
Yes, for video calls, recipes and visual control of the smart home. The Nest Hub is the best option.
