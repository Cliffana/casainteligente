What is a smart home?

A smart home is a home equipped with internet-connected devices that can be remotely controlled or automated. From turning on the lights with a voice command to automatically adjusting the thermostat when you leave the house, smart home technology makes your everyday life more practical, efficient and safe.

The smart home market in Portugal grew by more than 35% in 2025, with more and more Portuguese people discovering the advantages of home automation. And the good news? Getting started has never been so affordable.

Where to start? Budget and priorities

Before purchasing your first device, answer three questions:

  1. What is your initial budget? — We recommend starting with €50 to €100
  2. Which virtual assistant do you prefer? — Alexa (Amazon), Google Assistant or Apple HomeKit
  3. What problem do you want to solve first? — Lighting, security, energy saving?

Tip: Start small. An Amazon Echo Dot and a smart socket are enough to feel the impact of automation in your daily life.

Smart speaker — the center of your ecosystem

The smart speaker is the most important device. It serves as a voice assistant, control hub and music speaker. The Amazon Echo Dot 5th generation is our recommendation for beginners due to its excellent quality-price ratio.

Smart plugs — the gateway

With a smart plug, you can turn any “dumb” device into a smart device. Remotely turn on and off, create schedules and monitor energy consumption. The TP-Link Tapo P115 is the best entry-level option.

Protocols: WiFi, Zigbee, Z-Wave and Matter

Understanding the protocols is essential to make the right choices:

  • WiFi — Devices connect directly to your network. Easy to set up, but consumes more bandwidth.
  • Zigbee — Low-power mesh network. Needs a hub, but is more reliable for many devices.
  • Z-Wave — Similar to Zigbee, but less common in Europe.
  • Matter — The new universal standard. Matter devices work with any assistant.

Step by step: configure the first device

  1. Buy your Echo Dot on Amazon.es for the best price
  2. Plug in the power and wait for the orange LED
  3. Open the Alexa app on your smartphone and follow the instructions
  4. Connect to your WiFi network when the app asks
  5. Take the first test: "Alexa, what time is it?"
  6. Add a smart outlet and say "Alexa, discover devices"
  7. Create a routine: "Alexa, good night" → turn off lights and lock doors

Simple automations to get started

  • Good night: One command turns off all lights and sockets
  • Good morning: Lights up gently and gives the weather forecast
  • When leaving home: Turn off devices and activate away mode
  • Arrival home: Turn on entrance lights and deactivate alarm

Common beginner mistakes

  • Buying many devices at once without testing the ecosystem
  • Ignore cross-brand compatibility
  • Do not update device firmware
  • Place all devices on the same network without segmentation
  • Forget security: use strong passwords and 2FA whenever possible

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to start a smart home?
You can start for less than €50 with an Amazon Echo Dot and a smart socket. A complete setup costs between €200 and €500.
Do I need internet for a smart home?
Yes, most devices require WiFi. Some use Zigbee or Bluetooth but need a hub connected to the internet.
Is it worth investing in a smart home?
Yes, especially for safety, energy saving and everyday convenience.
