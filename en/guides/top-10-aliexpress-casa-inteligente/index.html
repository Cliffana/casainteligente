Amazon vs AliExpress — Which one to choose?
**Amazon**: Fast delivery (1-2 days), easy returns, slightly higher prices. Ideal for urgent products or Western brands (Philips Hue, Echo Dot). **AliExpress**: lower prices (20-50% less), slower delivery (10-20 days), native Chinese brands (Aqara, Sonoff, Yeelight). Ideal for sensors, automation kits and Xiaomi brands. The ideal strategy: use **Amazon for urgent essentials** and **AliExpress to build the ecosystem** (sensors, hubs, advanced automation).

Frequently Asked Questions

Is AliExpress safe to buy smart home products?
Yes, but always check the sales number and seller ratings. Prefer sellers with more than 95% positive feedback and thousands of sales. AliExpress has buyer protection — if you don't receive the product, you get a refund.
How long does delivery from AliExpress take to Portugal?
Normally 10-20 days with standard shipping (AliExpress Standard Shipping). Priority shipping (DHL, UPS) can arrive in 5-7 days. Always check the estimated deadline before purchasing.
Do AliExpress products work with Alexa and Google Home?
Most of the products listed do. Aqara sensors need the respective Zigbee hub. WiFi sockets and lamps connect directly. Check compatibility in each product description.
