Introduction

Xiaomi (through its sub-brand Aqara) and Tuya (through the Smart Life app) are two of the largest smart home ecosystems in the world. Both offer dozens of devices at affordable prices, but with different approaches. In this comparison, we analyze everything you need to know to choose the best one for you.

Xiaomi/Aqara: advantages and disadvantages

Xiaomi entered the smart home market through Aqara, focusing on build quality and design.

Pros: - Premium materials (glass, aluminum, high quality plastic) - Minimalist and elegant design - Very stable Zigbee gateway - Accurate and fast sensors - Robust integration with Home Assistant

Cons: - Slightly higher price - Less variety of products - Mi Home app can be confusing - Chinese cloud dependency on some devices

Tuya/Smart Life: advantages and disadvantages

Tuya is an open platform that dozens of brands use, resulting in a wide range of products.

Pros: - Very competitive prices - Wide range of devices - Intuitive Smart Life App - Works with Alexa, Google and Siri - Supports Matter on the latest devices

Cons: - Variable build quality - Design not always premium - Cloud dependency for many functions - Inconsistent customer support

Compatibility with Alexa, Google and HomeKit

With Alexa: Both work perfectly. Xiaomi requires the "Xiaomi Home" or "Aqara Home" skill. Tuya uses the "Smart Life" skill.

With Google Home: Works well on both. Xiaomi needs to link Mi Home account. Tuya uses Smart Life account.

With HomeKit: Xiaomi Aqara has hubs compatible with HomeKit. Tuya offers some devices with native HomeKit support.

Home Assistant: which one works best?

For Home Assistant users:

  • Xiaomi Aqara: Integration via Zigbee gateway is excellent. The Xiaomi Gateway 3 addon allows complete local control without the cloud.
  • Tuya: The official Tuya integration for Home Assistant works but has limited API quotas. The local alternative (Tuya Local) works for some WiFi devices.

Verdict: Xiaomi Aqara wins for Home Assistant for the most stable local integration.

Which one to choose in 2026?

Choose Xiaomi Aqara if: - Enhances construction quality - Use Home Assistant - Prefer premium design - Want more accurate sensors

Choose Tuya if: - Has a limited budget - Want a greater variety of products - Prefer the Smart Life app - Want to try it without a big investment Both ecosystems are excellent. The good news is that you don't need to choose just one — you can mix devices and use whichever assistant you prefer to control everything.

Frequently Asked Questions

Xiaomi or Tuya: which is cheaper?
The Xiaomi Aqara tends to be slightly more expensive but with better build quality. Tuya offers more low-cost options.
Which works best with Home Assistant?
Both work well, but the Xiaomi Aqara via Zigbee gateway has more stable integration with Home Assistant.
Can I mix Xiaomi and Tuya devices?
Yes, as long as they both support the same assistant (Alexa, Google Home). For local automations, you need a compatible hub.
