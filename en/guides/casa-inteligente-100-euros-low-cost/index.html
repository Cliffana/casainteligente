Why Start with €100?

€100 is the ideal budget to enter the world of smart home. With this value you can:

  • Smart lighting in 2 rooms
  • Control of an appliance
  • Temperature monitoring
  • Everything controllable by voice and cell phone

And the best part: no work, electrician or technical knowledge required.

3 Automations You Can Create with This Kit

1. Automatic Lights When You Arrive Home

Need: Zigbee Kit (motion sensor + lamp) Rule: If the door sensor detects an opening after 6pm → turns on the room light at 50%

2. Automatic Good Morning

Need: WiFi Kit (bedroom lamp + Alexa) Rule: "Alexa, good morning" → gradually turns on the room light for 5 minutes

3. Energy Saving

Need: Tapo P115 Rule: If the socket detects consumption > 0W for more than 2 hours → turn it off (avoid devices on standby)

What NOT to Buy (Yet)

With €100, avoid these expenses that don’t bring immediate value:

  • ❌ Security cameras — good cameras cost €30+. Leave it for the next budget
  • ❌ Smart locks — expensive (€80+) and complex installation
  • ❌ Smart thermostats — only compensate if you have central heating
  • ❌ "All-inclusive" kits — generally more expensive than buying separately

Step by Step to Start Today

  1. Choose the kit (WiFi or Zigbee)
  2. Buy the products from the links above
  3. Install the app (Tapo / Aqara) on your phone
  4. Connect the first product — follow the app instructions
  5. Set up routines — start with “automatic lights”
  6. Expand — add more devices as you need
Final Recommendation
For **90% of people**, the €91 WiFi Kit is sufficient and easier. Start with the light bulbs and sockets. When you feel like you want sensors, buy the Aqara Hub and add sensors. The important thing is to **start**. Even with 1 lamp and 1 socket (€25) you can already feel the difference. Automating your home is addictive — in 1 month you’ll want more.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is €100 enough for a smart home?
Yes, but it's a starter kit focused on 1-2 divisions. With €100 you can get smart lighting + sockets + sensors for the living room and bedroom. For the entire house, expect to spend €200-400 over a few months.
Can I expand later?
Yes, all suggested products are modular. You can add more lamps, sensors and sockets as your budget allows. Start small and expand.
Does it work with Alexa and Google Home?
Everything we recommend works with Alexa and Google Home. Aqara sensors require the hub, which is included in the quote for the Zigbee option.
